Shree Rohit Seva Sangh, Mumbai

The Gujarati Hindu Rohit Samaj Survey.

  • We are conducting this survey to gain deeper insights into the Rohit Samaj Community.
  • Our aim is to understand the professional and educational landscape within our community, especially focusing on the youth.
  • By identifying strengths in various fields and occupations, we hope to build a supportive network that will benefit our youth in finding jobs, mentorship, and guidance.
  • This survey will also help us connect older members of the community, fostering connections among those who live in close proximity. Through these efforts, we seek to build a stronger, more connected community for mutual growth and support.

Terms and Conditions

  • This survey is conducted solely for the purpose of gathering information to benefit the Gujarati Hindu Rohit Samaj Community.
  • All responses are voluntary, and you may choose to skip questions that you do not wish to answer.
  • Your personal data will be treated with strict confidentiality and will not be shared publicly without your consent.
  • Data collected will be used to create insights and connections within the community, potentially aiding in professional and social networking.
  • By participating in this survey, you agree to allow the organizers to use the information provided to develop resources for community betterment.
  • Participants should provide accurate information to ensure the effectiveness of the survey outcomes.
  • The organizers reserve the right to update these terms as needed. Any changes will be communicated accordingly.
